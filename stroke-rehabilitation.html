Stroke Rehabilitation

Helping You Regain Independence

Stroke rehabilitation begins after medical stabilisation and focuses on restoring movement, balance, strength and daily function. Every rehabilitation plan is tailored to the individual's abilities, goals and medical condition.

  • Mobility & Walking Training
  • Balance & Fall Prevention
  • Strength & Endurance
  • Positioning & Joint Care
  • Functional Independence
  • Caregiver Education

Recovery Journey

Clinic & Home Rehabilitation

Clinic rehabilitation provides access to structured therapy and equipment, while home rehabilitation focuses on real-life activities such as transfers, walking and home safety. The right option depends on the individual's goals, endurance and safety.

Caregiver Education

Families receive guidance on positioning, mobility assistance, transfer techniques, mobility aids and creating safe routines that encourage independence.

Emergency Warning

New facial drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty, sudden vision changes or severe headache require immediate emergency medical attention.
