About Home Visits

Personalised Physiotherapy in Your Home

Home physiotherapy is ideal for selected patients who have difficulty travelling, require rehabilitation within their home environment or need caregiver education. Each visit is tailored to the patient's mobility, goals and home setting.

  • Home Environment Assessment
  • Mobility & Walking Training
  • Balance & Fall Prevention
  • Transfer Training
  • Caregiver Education
  • Progress Monitoring
