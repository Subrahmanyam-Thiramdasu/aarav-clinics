Complete family therapy care in Hyderabad

Aarav Clinics was founded on a simple belief — that expert physiotherapy and child development care should feel personal, accessible and genuinely caring for every family.

Our Story

Built around the people we treat

What began as a single physiotherapy practice in Ashok Nagar has grown into a full family therapy centre serving patients from across Hyderabad. Over 15 years we have helped more than 10,000 children and adults move better, communicate clearly and live more independently.

Our multidisciplinary team works together — physiotherapists, speech-language pathologists, occupational therapists and child development specialists — so that your care is coordinated rather than fragmented.

Our Values

What Guides Our Care

Compassion First

Every patient is treated with empathy, respect, and kindness— just as we would care for our own family.

Evidence-Based Care

Every treatment plan follows modern clinical guidelines, proven techniques, and continuous medical research.

Whole-Family Care

From children to seniors, we provide personalized care for every stage of life under one trusted clinic.
