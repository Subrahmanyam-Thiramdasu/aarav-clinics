Pain After Physiotherapy

Mild temporary soreness may occur, but persistent worsening, dizziness, severe headache, increasing numbness or weakness should be reported immediately.

When to Seek Urgent Care

Seek urgent medical attention after major trauma or if neck pain is associated with sudden weakness, severe headache, fainting, difficulty speaking or swallowing, fever or rapidly worsening neurological symptoms.
