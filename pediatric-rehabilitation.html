About

Supporting Every Child's Development

Pediatric rehabilitation supports children experiencing movement, strength, balance, coordination, posture or developmental challenges. Therapy is goal-based, child-centred and adapted to each child's developmental level and family priorities.

  • Play-Based Therapy
  • Movement Training
  • Balance & Coordination
  • Strength Development
  • Caregiver Education
  • Home Activity Programmes

Therapy Approach

How Therapy is Delivered

Sessions may include play, strengthening, movement challenges, walking practice, positioning, caregiver education and practical home activities. Goals are reviewed regularly and adapted as your child develops.

Multidisciplinary Support

When appropriate, physiotherapy works alongside speech therapy, occupational therapy, behavioural and medical services using coordinated goals.

Parent & Caregiver Role

Families are partners in therapy. We provide practical home guidance designed to fit naturally into everyday routines.
