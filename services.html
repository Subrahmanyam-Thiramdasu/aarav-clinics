Our therapy & rehabilitation services

From physiotherapy and stroke rehabilitation to speech therapy and child development, Aarav Clinics offers complete care for children and adults in Hyderabad.

ABA-Therapy

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) uses observation and structured teaching to help children develop meaningful communication, daily living, learning and safety skills. Therapy is coordinated with communication, sensory, educational and medical needs.

Back Pain Physiotherapy

Back pain can affect sitting, bending, lifting, walking and sleep. At Aarav Clinics, we perform a detailed assessment before creating a rehabilitation programme tailored to your goals and daily routine.
