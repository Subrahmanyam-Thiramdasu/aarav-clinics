About Aarav Clinics

Complete family therapy care under one roof

Aarav Clinics is a trusted physiotherapy clinic and child development centre in Hyderabad. From a newborn reaching their first milestones to an adult recovering from surgery or a stroke, our multidisciplinary team designs care around the whole family.
